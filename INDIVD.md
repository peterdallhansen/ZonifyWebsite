**One-line positioning  
**Indivd is a privacy-first visitor analytics platform for physical locations that turns existing 2D cameras into a cloud-based source of "online-style" insight - helping retailers, malls, and airports measure audience quality, prove what works, and make better commercial decisions \[S1\]\[S2\]\[S3\].

# Executive summary

- What Indivd sells: a unified analytics platform for physical environments, using existing cameras to measure movement, engagement, funnels, dwell, audience quality, and business impact rather than relying on basic footfall alone \[S1\]\[S2\].
- Who it is for: retailers, shopping centres / mall operators, and airports; with tailored messaging for marketing, operations, experience/design, and leasing/tenant-review stakeholders \[S2\]\[S4\]\[S5\]\[S6\].
- Core promise: move from traffic volume to visitor value. Indivd repeatedly frames the product around "ideal visitors," value-based benchmarking, and proving the uplift from campaigns, layout changes, staffing, and tenant mix decisions \[S4\]\[S7\]\[S8\]\[S9\].
- Key proof points used on-site: 99.11% KPMG-validated accuracy; 1-3 ms anonymization; 5 patents; 7 years of research; no new hardware required; enterprise-grade security; EU-hosted infrastructure; and a stated 99.3% SLA \[S2\]\[S3\].
- Why sales should care: the company's strongest commercial angle is not "people counting"; it is giving physical businesses the kind of actionable, attributable analytics that digital teams already expect - then tying that to revenue, ROI, staff planning, leasing confidence, or campaign proof \[S1\]\[S2\]\[S4\]\[S7\].

# What Indivd actually does

Indivd presents itself as a unified analytics platform that helps organizations understand how people move and interact in physical spaces. The company's messaging deliberately shifts the category from "people counting" to business intelligence for physical environments, with emphasis on true demand, funnels, bounce rates, dwell, visitor journeys, audience quality, and ROI \[S1\]\[S2\].

The product narrative is built around the idea that traditional footfall hides what matters. In Indivd's framing, raw traffic is not enough because it does not distinguish high-value visitors from low-value ones, does not explain why changes worked, and does not create fair comparisons across locations, campaigns, or tenants \[S4\]\[S5\]\[S6\]\[S7\]\[S8\]\[S9\].

**Primary problem / solution map**

| **Problem Indivd highlights**              | **What customers do today**                                                          | **Indivd answer**                                                                                                              |
| ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Traffic hides performance                  | Teams judge success by overall footfall, overall sales, or channel reach             | Measure "ideal visitors," visitor value, engagement, and uplift to see which actions attract the right audiences \[S4\]\[S7\]. |
| Benchmarks are misleading                  | Sites, zones, and campaigns are compared as if all visitors were equal               | Benchmark on value rather than volume, adjusting comparisons for audience quality \[S4\]\[S5\]\[S6\]\[S7\].                    |
| Changes are hard to attribute              | Layouts, staffing, campaigns, and displays are changed without clear proof of effect | Track before/after uplift and value growth to prove what actually works \[S4\]\[S7\]\[S8\].                                    |
| Leasing and tenant decisions lack evidence | Mall operators depend on sales and traffic but cannot prove tenant quality or fit    | Connect audience quality with tenant performance to support rent fairness, incentives, and mix strategy \[S6\]\[S9\].          |

# Capabilities and use cases

Across the site, the same core engine is repackaged into practical decision workflows. For sales purposes, it helps to describe Indivd as one platform with multiple monetizable use cases rather than a menu of disconnected features \[S2\]\[S3\].

- Track performance: identify which visitors matter most, benchmark by value, and measure whether changes create real impact \[S13\].
- Measure campaigns: see which campaigns attract and convert ideal visitors; benchmark campaign quality; estimate ROI by quality rather than traffic \[S7\].
- Test changes: evaluate store or space changes by their effect on ideal visitors and visitor value, not just raw lift \[S8\].
- Plan staff: align staffing to live demand and high-value visitor moments instead of generic traffic patterns \[S12\].
- Review tenants: connect audience quality to tenant performance, lease negotiations, incentives, and tenant mix \[S9\].
- Benchmark locations: compare stores, centres, terminals, and campaigns on audience quality and commercial relevance, not just totals \[S4\]\[S5\]\[S6\].

# Differentiators sales should lead with

- Not just footfall: Indivd's strongest differentiation is conceptual. It reframes the conversation from counting people to understanding which visitors create value and why \[S2\]\[S4\]\[S5\].
- Existing-camera deployment: the site repeatedly states that no new hardware is required; the platform connects to existing 2D cameras and can be live within days \[S2\]\[S3\].
- Privacy-by-design story: anonymization in 1-3 ms before data hits storage, no biometric data, no customer data used for training, 100+ DPIAs, and a prior consultation with the Swedish supervisory authority \[S3\]\[S14\].
- Proof orientation: KPMG-validated 99.11% accuracy is a major trust marker and should be used early in enterprise conversations \[S2\]\[S3\].
- Enterprise readiness: REST API, enterprise data-pipeline integration, custom IDs, real-time / aggregated / scheduled data delivery, and EU-hosted infrastructure with a stated 99.3% SLA \[S3\]\[S4\].
- Cloud-native economics: the technology page positions Indivd as upgrading current camera infrastructure into an analytics network rather than forcing a rip-and-replace project \[S3\].

# Messaging framework for pitches

Recommended short pitch

Indivd helps physical businesses measure their spaces the way digital businesses measure websites. Using the cameras you already have, it shows not just how many people came, but which visitors created value, what actions changed behavior, and where to invest next \[S1\]\[S2\]\[S3\].

Recommended medium pitch

Most physical-space analytics stop at footfall. Indivd goes further. It turns existing 2D cameras into a privacy-first analytics layer that helps retailers, malls, and airports understand audience quality, benchmark locations fairly, prove campaign and layout impact, and make better commercial decisions with enterprise-ready accuracy and compliance \[S2\]\[S3\]\[S4\]\[S5\]\[S6\].

# Open questions sales should qualify live

The public site is strong on positioning and proof points, but lighter on certain buying details. A seller should plan to qualify these areas in discovery rather than assume them.

- Pricing structure is not publicly detailed on the pages reviewed. Expect custom pricing and solution scoping.
- The public site does not clearly enumerate packaged modules, seat models, or camera-based pricing bands.
- Customer logos and named case studies appear limited on the pages reviewed, so reps may need additional private references for late-stage enterprise procurement.
- Deployment responsibilities, implementation timelines by complexity, and integration depth by system are framed broadly rather than in technical detail on the reviewed pages.

# Bottom-line sales take

Indivd is best pitched as a privacy-first analytics platform that gives physical businesses the measurement sophistication digital teams already take for granted. The most persuasive commercial thread is not "better people counting"; it is "better commercial decisions through better understanding of audience quality, causality, and value." That message is consistently reinforced across the marketing, product, technology, retail, airport, mall, performance, campaign, and tenant-review pages \[S1\]\[S2\]\[S3\]\[S4\]\[S5\]\[S6\]\[S7\]\[S9\]\[S13\].
